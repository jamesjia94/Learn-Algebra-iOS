Terms

4.1

Polynomial: an algebraic expression that contains multiple terms with at least one variable-term that in them that have only whole number non-negative exponents

Degree of a Term: the value of the exponent attached to the variable; for example, the degree of \(3x^2\) is \(2\)

Degree of a Polynomial: the degree of the term in the polynomial with the highest degree; for example, the degree of \(9x^2+3x+5 \) is \(2\) because \(9x^2\) has the highest degree with degree \(2\)

Monomial: a single term polynomial

Binomial: a polynomial with two terms

Trinomial: a polynomial with three terms

Standard Form: \(a_n x^n+a_{(n-1)} x^{(n-1)}+\dots+a_1 x+a_0\)

Formulas

4.3

The FOIL Method: \( (a+b)(c+d) = ac + ad + bc + bd \)

Squaring a Sum Binomial: \((a+b)^2 = a^2 + 2ab + b^2\)

Squaring a Difference Binomial: \((a-b)^2 = a^2 - 2ab + b^2\)

Difference of Two Squares: \((a+b)(a-b) = a^2 - b^2\)

Sample Solutions

4.2

Adding Polynomials:

Add \(3x^2+5x+3\) and \(5x^2+x-1\)

Add them: \((3x^2+5x+3)+(5x^2+x-1)\Rightarrow 3x^2+5x^2+5x+x+3-1\)

Group terms with the same variables and simplify: \((3x^2+5x^2 )+(5x+x)+(3-1)\)

Answer: \(8x^2+6x+2\)

Subtracting Polynomials:

Subtract \(x^2-2x+3\) and \(4x^2+5x-6\)

Subtract them:\((x^2-2x+3)-(4x^2+5x-6)\Rightarrow x^2-4x^2-2x-5x+3+6\)

Group terms with the same variables and simplify:\((x^2-4x^2 )+(-2x-5x)+(3+6)\)

Answer: \(-3x^2-7x+9\)

4.3

Multiplying Monomials:

Multiply \(5x^2\) and \(9x\)

Multiply the coefficients and the variables separately: \((5)(9)*(x^2 )(x)\)

Simplify: \(45*x^{(2+1)}=45x^3\)

Answer: \(45x^3\)

Multiplying Binomials:

Multiply \((3x+5)(x-2)\)

Use the FOIL method: \((3x)(x)+(3x)(-2)+(5)(x)+(5)(-2)\)

Simplify: \(3x^2-6x+5x-10\Rightarrow 3x^2-x-10\)

Answer: \(3x^2-x-10\)

4.4

Dividing Monomials:

Divide: \(6x^2\) by \(3x\)

Divide the coefficients and variables separately: \( (\frac{6}{3})*(\frac{x^2}{x})\)

Simplify: \(2*x\)

Answer: \(2x\)

Dividing a Binomial by a Monomial:

Divide: \(3x^3+9x^2\) by \(3x\)

Divide each term of the binomial by the monomial: \( \frac{(3x^3)}{3x}+\frac{(9x^2)}{3x}\)

Simplify: \((\frac{3}{3}) x^{(3-1)}+(\frac{9}{3}) x^{(2-1)}\)

Answer: \(x^2+3x\)
